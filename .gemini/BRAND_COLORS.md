## Design Principles

### **Hierarchy**
1. **Primary Actions**: Orange (`#fe9900`) - Pay buttons, active states
2. **Brand Elements**: Deep Blue (`#122f8a`) - Header, navigation, borders
3. **Content**: White (`#ffffff`) - Cards and backgrounds
4. **Text**: Deep Blue/Dark Gray - Readable and professional

### **Consistency**
- All interactive elements use Deep Blue borders
- All primary actions use Orange
- All cards use White backgrounds
- All shadows use brand colors for cohesion

### **Accessibility**
- High contrast between text and backgrounds
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Consistent color meaning (green = positive, red = negative)
- Readable font sizes and weights
